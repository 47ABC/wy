我的世界(Minecraft)

我的世界(Minecraft)是一款风靡全球的游戏，目前销量在三亿左右。

我的世界(Minecraft)的发展之路。

我的世界(Minecraft)

2009-05
瑞典程序员 Markus “Notch” Persson 在 TIGSource 论坛放出首个公开版本 “Cave Game”，Java 编写，只支持放置/破坏方块。

2009-10
改名 Minecraft；加入生存模式、日夜循环、怪物。 2010-06 开启付费 Alpha，售价 9.95 €，官网多人在线购买系统上线。

2010-12
进入 Beta，售价 14.95 €；Redstone、成就、服务端插件接口雏形出现。

2011-11-18
MineCon 拉斯维加斯，1.0 正式版发布；同月正式版销量破 400 万。

2012-03
Xbox 360 版上线，首日销售 100 万份，主机市场大门被撬开。

2013-09
Mojang 把 Pocket Edition（安卓/iOS）推到 0.7，首次支持局域网联机；移动端开始爆发。

2014-09
微软宣布 25 亿美元收购 Mojang；Notch 与两位联合创始人退出。

2015-07
Windows 10 版（基于 C++ 的基岩引擎）发布，实现跨 Win10/Xbox/PE 联机，统一代码基线。

2016-06
“中国版”交给网易代理，开启不限号测试，国际服与中国服正式分流。

2017-09
1.12 “World of Color” 之后，Java 版与基岩版开始同步大版本号；同年 Switch 版发售。

2018-10
“水域更新”1.13 完成，海洋系统彻底重做；销量破 1.5 亿份，成史上第二畅销游戏。

2019-04
“村庄与掠夺”1.14 上线，重写村民 AI 与交易体系；同年 Minecraft Earth AR 手游短暂试水。

2020-06
“下界更新”1.16 把地狱变成可生存维度；教育版因疫情全球免费，月活突破 1.3 亿。

2021-06
“洞穴与山崖”1.17/1.18 拆分发布，建筑高度扩至 Y=-64~319，重写世界生成器；销量达 2.38 亿份。

2022-06
“荒野更新”1.19 加入深暗之域、监守者、Warden；Minecraft Dungeons 停更，Mojang 转向动作战略新作。

2023-06
“足迹与故事”1.20 主打考古、樱花木、饰纹陶罐；官方启动器统一 Java/基岩入口。

2024-10

销量正式突破 3 亿份，月活 1.8 亿，成为人类历史上最畅销的单体游戏；官方路线图公布 1.21 “Tricky Trials” 试炼大厅与风力挑战，继续无终更新模式。

Minecraft

Minecraft is a global phenomenon, with sales of around 300 million copies.

Minecraft Development Timeline

2009-05
Swedish programmer Markus “Notch” Persson released the first public version “Cave Game” on TIGSource forum, written in Java, only place/destroy blocks.

2009-10
Renamed Minecraft; added Survival mode, day/night cycle, mobs.

2010-06
Paid Alpha opened, priced at 9.95 €, online purchase system launched.

2010-12
Entered Beta, priced at 14.95 €; Redstone, achievements, early server plug-in API.

2011-11-18
MineCon Las Vegas, version 1.0 released; sales exceeded 4 million that month.

2012-03
Xbox 360 edition launched, 1 million copies sold on day one, opening the console market.

2013-09
Mojang pushed Pocket Edition (Android/iOS) to 0.7, first LAN support; mobile segment began to explode.

2014-09
Microsoft acquired Mojang for 2.5 billion USD; Notch and two co-founders left.

2015-07
Windows 10 Edition (C++ Bedrock engine) released, enabling cross-play among Win10/Xbox/PE, unified codebase.

2016-06
“China Edition” handed to NetEase, unlimited open beta started; international and Chinese servers split.

2017-09
After 1.12 “World of Color”, Java and Bedrock editions began synchronized version numbers; Switch edition launched the same year.

2018-10
“Update Aquatic” 1.13 completed, ocean system rebuilt; sales hit 150 million, becoming the second best-selling game in history.

2019-04
“Village & Pillage” 1.14 went live, villagers AI and trading system rewritten; Minecraft Earth AR mobile game briefly tested the same year.

2020-06
“Nether Update” 1.16 turned the Nether into a survivable dimension; Education Edition became free worldwide due to COVID-19, MAU exceeded 130 million.

2021-06
“Caves & Cliffs” 1.17/1.18 split release, build height expanded to Y=-64~319, world generator rewritten; sales reached 238 million.

2022-06
“Wild Update” 1.19 added Deep Dark, Warden; Minecraft Dungeons stopped updates, Mojang shifted to new action-strategy title.

2023-06
“Trails & Tales” 1.20 focused on archaeology, cherry wood, decorated pots; official launcher unified Java/Bedrock entry.

2024-10
Sales officially exceeded 300 million, MAU 180 million, becoming the best-selling single game in history; roadmap 1.21 “Tricky Trials” trial chambers & wind challenges announced, endless updates continue.
